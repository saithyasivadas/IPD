About IPD

Internship and Project Division (IPD) is an inspiring initiative by SSERD to bring creative and scientific minds among students, to convert their substantial ideas into reality and to deliver an actual revolutionary product for the betterment of the society and science.The main focus of SSERD has been to revolutionise the Research and Development (R&D) sector in India, IPD contributes by becoming the first stepping stone for the organisation to do so.Also at IPD to add charm to our projects we make sure that the students are guided by the best available experts not only in India but also from abroad.

Target Students:

  • B.Tech/B.E
  • Diploma/B.Sc.
  • School Students.
Duration : 3 MONTHS
MODE : ONLINE
